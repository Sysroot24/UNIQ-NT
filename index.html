About the Developer

Hello, my name is Ankit Thakur, and I am a passionate web developer. I created this website specifically for CBSE Class 10 students to provide them with a resourceful platform where they can access important materials for their board exams.

As a web developer, I have worked on various projects, but this one holds a special place for me as it is focused on helping students in their crucial years of schooling. This website offers access to important questions, NCERT exemplar problems, previous year questions (PYQs), and project works that will help students prepare effectively for their exams.

The primary goal of this website is to offer a comprehensive, easy-to-use platform where students can find study materials related to the Class 10 CBSE curriculum. I have designed this website with a user-friendly interface, ensuring that students can easily navigate and find what they need.

I believe in the power of education and am committed to supporting students in their academic journey. I hope this website proves to be a valuable resource for all the hardworking CBSE Class 10 students out there!
